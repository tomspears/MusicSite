Lessons

Everything I teach is uniquely tailored to each student. Everyone comes from their own background with a unique set of experience already in place, and no singular, rigid, curriculum can deliver this to every student with success. Together, we craft a learning path that encompasses all necessary knowledge and skill, but we will approach it in a way you understand, at your own pace.

Technique

Picking, chops, speed, accuracy, the skills that allow you to play with confidence. Your unique voice on the instrument comes from the infinite variables in 'how' you play: the pick's attack on the string, where along the string you pick, the shape of your vibrato. Elements of technique are broken down and the skills you are most interested in are focused on.

Music Theory

Scales, Chords - Melody, Harmony and Rhythm - these are the gears that we can examine to understand the sounds that we hear in music. To me, music theory is like the 'recipe' for a sound - there for you to learn from so you can play with the recipe and find your own path in the universe of music. Styles add diversity to the sounds we hear - it is why country music and jazz have difference vocabularies, much like the difference between Cajun and Nordic cuisine.

Repertoire

Learn your favorite songs with custom transcriptions and video guides of your favorite artists. The whole point here is to have fun and develop a musical vocabulary. Music is not made in to stay in a bubble, and through learning your favorite songs you will learn to appy the concepts in our lessons to real life music.
